## The "Mesh-War" (مشوار) Vision
The next evolution for **Spark Systems** is wireless intelligence. While Meteor audits the *host*, Mesh-War will audit the *airwaves*.

### Integration Strategy:
1.  **SDR Provider**: We will implement a new Provider for Software Defined Radio (SDR).
2.  **Packet Analysis**: Extending `scapy` usage for 802.11 management frames and Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) payloads.
3.  **Visual Mesh**: The Nebula Dashboard will be expanded to include "Signal Mapping" for wireless nodes.

## Preparing for Mesh-War (مشوار)
To prepare Meteor for this integration:
- Keep the `HeuristicEngine` agnostic to the source of data.
- Ensure the `KillChainAnalyzer` can handle "External Radio Interference" as a risk factor.

# Chapter 1: Strategic Mission & Identity 🛡️⚡

## The Spark Systems Philosophy
At **Spark Systems** (برق للأنظمة), we believe that security is not a reactive state, but a proactive intelligence cycle. Our tools are designed to provide "Tactical Dominance" over the digital environment.

## Meteor: The Tactical Probe
**Meteor** is our first strike in this cycle. It is designed to be:
1.  **Stealthy**: Using SYN scans and local-first analysis to minimize noise.
2.  **Intelligent**: Not just listing ports, but correlating them to processes and external threat signatures.
3.  **Extensible**: A foundation for future intelligence modules.

## The "Mesh-War" (مشوار) Project
Our roadmap leads to **Mesh-War**—a revolutionary project for wireless cyber intelligence. Meteor provides the ground-level system intelligence, while Mesh-War will extend our "eyes" to the airwaves (WiFi, Bluetooth, SDR). 

The architecture we build today in Meteor (The Provider Pattern) is exactly what will allow Mesh-War to interact with diverse wireless hardware tomorrow.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> Every line of code written for Spark Systems must adhere to the principle of **High Cohesion** and **Low Coupling**. We build for the future.
